## Welcome

Welcome to the Math@Illinois wiki! Here, you can find resources for specific classes at UIUC or any tips relating to math. Currently, we have pages on [[classes/]] and [[research/]].

Maintained by students from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.

Join our community on [Discord](https://discord.gg/Fds22YywnA)!

## Contributing

Whether you're a math major, or just someone who has taken a math course, you can contribute too! Any updates, from fixing a typo to adding a page for a class, are welcome.

View more on contributing [here](https://github.com/3nya/math-at-illinois/blob/main/CONTRIBUTING.md).
